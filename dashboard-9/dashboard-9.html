1. Trend Analysis and Forecasting
- What It Does: Uses historical data to predict future bookings.
- How It Helps: Forecasts total bookings for the year, enabling informed decisions and resource planning.
- Example Insight: "Bookings are expected to increase by [X%] over the next six months."
2. Growth Rate Comparison
- What It Does: Compares bookings month-over-month and year-over-year.
- How It Helps: Reveals growth or decline trends, helping evaluate marketing effectiveness.
- Example Insight: "Bookings in February 2025 are up [X%] from January 2025 and [X%] from February 2024."
3. Anomaly Detection and Insights
- What It Does: Identifies unusual booking patterns and potential causes.
- How It Helps: Pinpoints factors influencing bookings, such as promotions or external events.
- Example Insight: "A booking spike in February 2025 was linked to a promotion campaign."
